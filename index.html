Services: 

We'll work with you closely to fully understand your project requirements, from what you want to communicate to the style in which you'd like to do it. We'll then come up with a proposal, budget and time-frame that works for you.

This is when things get exciting: you'll be involved in scripting, rehearsals and costume fitting, make-up trials and cinematography testing.

Often the most fun part of film making. We are up against the clock and at the mercy of the elements, however this is where the magic happens. And sometimes, when things come together, it is like watching god's hands at play.

The editing is actually the crafting of your film. Assembling sound and video, then grading and colouring to create a moving experience that can be shared with countless people.
